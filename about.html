Aim

To develop qualities of character, courage, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, spirit of adventure and sportsmanship and the ideas of selfless service among the youth of the country to make them useful citizens.

To create a human resource of organised, trained and motivated youth to provide leadership in all walks of life including the armed forces and be always available for the service of the nation.

Motto

Be Disciplined and punctual.

Make no excuses and tell no lies.

Work hard and without fuss.

Obey with a smile.

Oath

"I do hereby solemnly promise that I will serve my motherland most truly and loyally and that, I will abide by the rules and regulations of the National Cadet Corps. Further under the command and control of my commanding officer I will participate in every camp most sincerely and wholeheartedly."

Pledge

We, the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizen of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern of our fellow beings.
